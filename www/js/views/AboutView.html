By clicking on the feature you can select the feature(s) that matches the bumblebee to narrow down your search...

Step 4:

Step 5:

You can see your current location on the map but if you took the photo of bumblebee in different place, you can write it in ADDRESS box.

You can also give us an extra information about the place e.g. forest, farmland, garden, hedgerows or mountains.

There is also a box where you can write an extra comment before you SUBMIT IDENTIFICATION.

Again here you can post the picture you took on you FB wall.
